Simply Rugby Club

Desktop Management System - User Manual

Welcome to the new Simply Rugby Desktop Management System. This manual is designed to help club staff (Secretaries and Coaches) navigate the core features for efficient and compliant club operations.

1. User Roles & Access Control

This system strictly enforces Separation of Duty. Your login credentials determine which modules you can access on the sidebar:

  • Coach: Focuses on technical and on-pitch activities. Has access to Training Skills, Attendance, Match Records, and Medical Records.
  • Secretary: Focuses on administration and compliance. Has access to Member Directory, Safeguarding, and Training Schedule.
Note: If certain buttons are hidden from your sidebar, it is a deliberate security measure ensuring you only access modules relevant to your role.

2. Navigating the Interface

  • Left Navigation Sidebar: Your main menu for accessing different business modules.
  • Main Content Area: The central workspace where data grids and input forms are displayed.
  • Bottom Status Bar: Displays your current login role and real-time system notifications.

3. Core Modules Guide

📊 System Dashboard

The default landing page providing a real-time overview of club KPIs, including Total Registered Players, Currently Injured Players (synced with Medical Records), and Upcoming Training Sessions.

👥 Members & Safeguarding

  • Member Directory: Manage player profiles, SRU registration numbers, and contact details.
  • Junior Safeguarding: Legal Compliance Requirement. Automatically filters players under 18 (U18). You must register their legal guardian details, emergency contacts, and pickup authorizations here.

🏉 Training & Performance

  • Training Skills: Standardized assessment module where coaches rate core skills (Passing, Tackling, Kicking) across different difficulty levels (Standard, Spin, Pop).
  • Attendance Tracking: Log whether players were Present, Absent, or Excused to track commitment levels.

🏆 Match Records

Record all fixture results. Includes symmetrical scoring inputs for both the 1st Half and Final Score (Us vs. Them), along with coach performance summaries.

🏥 Medical & Injury Records

Log injury types, severity, and treatments.
Clearance Protocol: A player's status will remain "Injured" on the Dashboard until the "Cleared to Play" checkbox is officially ticked by authorized staff.

📅 Training Schedule

A resource planning tool for Secretaries to allocate pitches (e.g., Pitch 1, Indoor Gym), set times, and assign Lead Coaches for future sessions to prevent double-booking.

📈 Reports & Analytics

Generate visual trend reports for player progress and season win rates. Data can be exported to PDF or Excel for official club documentation.

4. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: Why didn't the injured player count increase on the Dashboard after I added a medical record?
A: Please ensure you did NOT check the "Cleared to Play" box when saving the record. Only active, non-recovered injuries are counted.
Q: Why can't I find a newly added player in the Safeguarding dropdown?
A: The Safeguarding module automatically calculates age based on the Date of Birth. Only players strictly under 18 years old will appear in this list.
Q: How do I reset my password?
A: For security reasons in the current version, all password resets must be handled directly by the System Administrator.
